🔧 CUSTOMIZATION

  Format: Q: question|A: answer
  Restart server and test

  Edit: src/main/java/chatbot/Chatbot.java
  Modify: generateRuleBasedResponse() method
  Recompile and restart

  Edit: web/css/style.css (colors, fonts, spacing)
  Edit: web/js/chatbot.js (behavior)
  Edit: index.html (structure)
  Refresh browser (no restart needed)
